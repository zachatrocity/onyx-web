hang about
#alpha

I built hang.live because the internet forgot how to hang out.

On one end of the spectrum, there's corporate hell. Join a meeting at the scheduled time and turn on your webcam just to pretend that you're paying attention. Does anybody actually like Zoom? Or have we collectively accepted that voice chats are not allowed to be fun?

On the other end of the spectrum, there's the feed. Watch some brainrot. Scroll some memes. Everything is so anonymous and fake. Nothing is live and half of your friends are probably bots.

hang.live is your new couch. Drop in, chat with friends, hang out as long as you want. No meetings, no agendas, no likes, no comments. Talk about dumb shit or just chill. I don't care.

This is an early alpha release and there's still a ton of work to do. Feel free to hang out but hold off sharing this site with your friends until accounts/rooms are implemented. It won't take long.

Tech

A lot of the cool shit on this site is only possible because of new browser technologies. That's right, we're not just edgy, but also bleeding edge.

This site uses Media over QUIC for live audio, video, chat, etc. If you're technically inclined, the core libraries are open source. We're not using WebRTC like everyone else so there's still some rough edges; feel free to contribute!

Want to ditch Zoom, Meet, Teams, Webex, etc? Enterprise support is coming eventually so hit me up if you're interested. Meetings should be social and dare I say: fun.
